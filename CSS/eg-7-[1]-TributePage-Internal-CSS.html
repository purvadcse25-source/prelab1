Shivaji Maharaj's Birth:


Date: February 19, 1630
Place: Shivneri Fort (near Pune)
Parents: Jijabai (mother) and Shahaji Bhosale (father)
Celebration: Officially celebrated on February 19 (Shivaji Jayanti)

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630–1680) was a warrior king and founder of the Maratha
Empire in western India. Born at Shivneri Fort, he pioneered guerrilla warfare (Ganimi Kava), established Hindavi Swarajya, and was crowned at Raigad Fort in
1674. He is celebrated for his progressive administration, establishment of a strong naval force, and secular policies that promoted justice and religious
tolerance.
