Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Born: February 19, 1630
Founder: Maratha Empire https://upload.wikimedia.org

Shivaji Maharaj was a great Indian warrior-king and a member of the Bhonsle Maratha clan. He is known for his bravery, military tactics, and establishing a progressive civil rule with the help of a disciplined military and well-structured administrative organizations.

He promoted the use of Marathi and Sanskrit in his court and fought bravely to protect his people and land.
